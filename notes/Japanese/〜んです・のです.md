- Behaves functionally similar to just だ・です by itself
- Strengthens the assertion of the relationship, adds an explanatory tone.


>[!warning] Remember This!
>You obviously can't be explanatory when asking a question. So when んですか is used, it is implying that further information is desired by the speaker.
>
>お腹が空いたんですか？*Are you hungry?*
