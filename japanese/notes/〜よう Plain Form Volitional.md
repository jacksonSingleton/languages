# Plain Form Volitional
---
- We have seen the polite volitional, as in "Let's read"
	- 読みましょう
- However, in plain form, it's concatenated to
	- 読もう
- For 一段  verbs, they take on よう at the end
	- 食べる→食べよう

> [!important] Tip
> Volitional forms may also be seen expanded as:
> **Polite**: 食べましょう→食べるでしょう
> **Plain:** 食べよう→食べるだろう

## Irregular Verbs
する→しましょう→しよう：Let's do...
来る→来ましょう「きましょう」→こよう：Let's come


> [!example] Examples
> Ex. 1
> 勉強しよう
>
> ---
> Let's study
> 
> Ex. 2
> 携帯を持ってこよう
> ---
> Let's bring our phones
